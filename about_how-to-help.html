Do you like this website, find it useful and like to help? There are some things you can do:

Images - most of the philatelic items shown on this website are from the collection of the Site Admin. Other images were shared by friends or visitors to the site. A few were downloaded from other sites on the internet.
If you came across a low quality image and have the stamp or any other philatelic item in your collection, please share scans of the stamps with the Site Admin (600 dpi preferred).
The same if you have some stamp or any other philatelic item related to Paleontology topic, that is missing on this website.

Information - this website provides not only images of stamp or any other philatelic item related to Paleontology or Paleoanthropology topic, but also try to describe every philatelic item listed on the site. So far (February 2022), over 300 description pages were created for official, local and personalized stamps as well as some commemorative covers.
The Site Admin of this site is constantly looking for some articles, old and new philatelic magazines or any other source of information about philatelic material represented in the Philatelic Catalog of this website.
The Site Admin is also permanently looking for information about coming and most recent stamp or any other philatelic item related to the topic of this website, to allow to visitor of this website to arrange new philatelic material in time.
If you have such information please send it to the Site Admin.

Errors correction - this website has almost 800 pages on the moment (February 2022), some pages were written a while ago, others are added just recently. Some pages are stable, others are under construction or updated permanently.
If you find any issue at particular page: display, spelling, dead link etc. - please don't hesitate to inform the Site Admin.

Feedback - you are very welcome to share your opinion about this website.
Do you think something have to be added or replaced?
Do you think there are something to be improved or just like to say "Hello"?
Your feedback is very welcome - you can contact the Site Admin on either English, Deutsch, or Russian languages, what ever of the languages you prefer.

Share with others - if you like this site please tell it to others - share some links to this site in social media, tell about it to other collectors and philatelic clubs.
If you have your-own website or blog, link exchange is very welcome.
As more people visit it and provide their feedback as more accurate and error free it will be.

Last but not least- this website is completely free for its visitors, but as you can imagine, on top of free-time spent by the Site Admin almost every day, there are some costs associated with it.
If you like the site, find it useful and like to support it - you are welcome to donate what ever amount you like on PayPal account of the Site Admin, or access Amazon stores through one of links distributed over the website.
Collection of some Paleophilately related books with links to international Amazon store can be found on the Madia page, for example.

Amazon will give some small % of every purchase to the site.
It doesn't cost you any more, but it really helps out here.
The price is the same, the difference is that a small percentage supports this site.
If you usually buy on the Amazon you can support this website without spending anything more than that expended by buying directly from an e-shops to which they are associated.
