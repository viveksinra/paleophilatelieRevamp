Paleophilately group is a place where you can chat online with me, as well as communicate with over 700 collectors from all around the world who share the same interest in collecting prehistoric life on stamps.

The Main pages of the website are

:
  • Stamp Catalog - sorted into two variations by Country name and in Chronological order, by release date.
  • List of Paleontology and Paleoanthropology related stamps, postmarks and postal stationeries, issued this year.
  • Image galleries of stamps, FDCs, postmarks and circulated covers. Scans of almost 3.000 items can be seen there, mostly in high resolution.
  • Description pages of stamps with direct connection to Paleontology - stamps depicting dinosaurs and other prehistoric animals and plants, fossils and paleontologists.
  • Description pages of stamps with indirect connection to Paleontology - stamps depicting fossil-found places, Natural History Museums with significant collection of fossils and contributor to Paleontology or Paleoanthropology science.
  • Exhibits Room - philatelic exhibitions of fellow collectors.
  • Collection of Paleophilately related Articles.
