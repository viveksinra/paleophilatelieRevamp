Contact details of the Author and Admin of Paleophilatelie.eu website

The Author and the Admin of PaleoPhilatelie.eu website is Mr. Michael Kogan also known on some Internet sites under his nickname: "Paleophilatelist".
Email: admin@paleophilatelie.eu

You can send your messages to Mr. Kogan in English, German, Ukrainian or Russian languages.


You are welcome to follow Mr. Michael Kogan on other internet platforms:
Facebook, Twitter, International philatelic forums.


Mr. Michael Kogan is member of the following philatelic clubs and societies:
  • American Topical Association (ATA)
  • Biophilately Study Unit of ATA, where he holds Associated Editor position for Paleontology division and maintain "Paleontology" new issues list, (short presentation of the club is here)
  • Bund Deutscher Philatelisten (BDPh) - German Philatelic Association
  • German philatelic club "Arbeitsgemeinschaft Bergbau und Geowissenschaften e. V" (short presentation of the club is here)
