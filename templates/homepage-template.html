About Paleophilatelie

Paleophilatelie.eu is an online resource combining Philately with Paleontology and Paleoanthropology. This website shows images and provides information about thousands of philatelic items related to Paleontology and Paleoanthropology from almost 200 countries and territories.

The website contains many hundreds of pages describing individual stamp issues and articles related to the "Paleophilatelie" topics, as well as many examples of philatelic exhibits.

If you are visiting the website for the first time, please read the "About Paleophilatelie.eu website" article to get an overview.
