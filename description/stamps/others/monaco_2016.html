Monaco 2016 "Centenary of excavations at Exotic Garden Cave"


<< previous page back to index next page >>

Issue Date 24.06.2016
ID Michel: 3303; Scott: 2852; Stanley Gibbons: 3219; Yvert et Tellier: 3045; Category: pF
Designer Yves BEAUJARD
Stamps in set 1
Value 1,60€ - Prince Albert I and excavation at Exotic Garden Cave
Emission/Type commemorative
Issue place Monaco
Size (width x height) 52mm x 31,77 mm
Layout Mini Sheets of 10 stamps
Products FDC x1 MS x1
Paper 3 colors steel-engraving
Perforation 12.75 x 13.25
Print Technique Offset, 3 colours steel-engraving
Printed by Phil@Poste
Quantity 450.000 stamps
Issuing Authority Office des Emissions de Timbres-poste, Principauté de Monaco

On June 24th, 2016, the Post of Monaco issued one more stamp of Albert I, Prince of Monaco.

The year 2016 marked the centenary of the first excavations at the Observatory Cave in Monaco’s Exotic Garden. Begun in 1916, these excavations produced a major discovery: evidence of the earliest signs of human occupation in Monaco.
Prince Albert I had long been convinced of the great archaeological potential of the site. The excavations revealed successive Palaeolithic occupations dating from 300,000 to 5,000 years ago.
