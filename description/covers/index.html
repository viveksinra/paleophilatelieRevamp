Articles about commemorative covers, postal stationeries which are related to Paleontology or Paleoanthropology

This page contains list of article pages with detailed description of commemorative covers, postal stationeries, related to Paleontology or Paleoanthropology science. Covers depicting dinosaurs, other prehistoric animals and plants, fossils and paleontologists on theirm cachets or imprinted stamps.
To date (August 2023), the list contains only few articles. To keep updated about new and modified articles of the website please keep looking at News page.

Other available list is:
  • Stamps which directly related to Paleontology or Paleoanthropology.
  • Stamps and covers depicting fossil-found places, Natural History Museums with significant collection of fossils, sculpturs of prehistoric animals and contributor to Paleontology or Paleoanthropology.
  • Personalized stamps directly related to Paleontology or Paleoanthropology

Please click on the issue title below to be redirected to the relevant page that contain image of the cover as well as technical details and some description of depicted objects.
Please click on the country name to be redirected to the country page, to see all philatelic items of the country related to "Paleophilatelie" subjects.
