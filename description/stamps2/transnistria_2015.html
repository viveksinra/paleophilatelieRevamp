On March 2015, post authority of Transnitria, issued a new set of stamps - stamps from 1995 overprinted text "Archeolofy of Transnitria Paleolit" stamps with gold and silver colour.

The series consists of five stamps depicting the life of Paleolithic man and objects found during archaeological excavations on the territory of Transnistria near the village Rashkov and in a grotto near the village Vyhvatintsy "- said the director of the State Unitary Enterprise" Mark Transnistria "Vsevolod Lawrence.
The series is made by applying overlay "Archaeology of Transnistria" by stamping silver and gold foil, its circulation - 5500 copies.
The presentation took place at the department of nature MU "Tiraspol united museum."
