What is Included?

This catalogue includes all kinds of philatelic items featuring dinosaurs and other prehistoric animals, early mankind, paleontologists, paleontologic and paleoanthropologic museums, fossils and fossil sites, and contributors to both sciences, including Charles Darwin.

What is Not Included?

  • Any kind of unofficial stamps including items that resemble postage stamps but were not issued by a postal authority.
  • Stamps depicting dinosaurs from cartoons (Flintstones, Disney, etc.), as objects of humor, children's artwork, or illustrations from fiction (Jules Verne, etc.).
