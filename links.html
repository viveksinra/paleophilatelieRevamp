Paleophilately related Thematical websites, blogs and clubs:

  • StampedOut - prehistoric life on stamps.
    A website that covers a large amount of philatelic material depicting prehistoric life. Includes a comprehensive list of extinct species on stamps.
  • Geology on postage stamps (last time updated in 2022)
    A significant selection of geologic postage stamps: rocks, minerals, fossils, mines, oil, etc...
  • Biology on stamps
    Multilanguage site about biology and paleontology related stamps.

Alternative stamp catalogs:
  • WNS - The WNS (for WADP Numbering System) is the worldwide numbering system for postage stamps created by the Universal Postal Union (UPU) and the World Association for the Development of Philately (WADP). It list stamps of many countries issued after 2002.
  • Stamps of the World (SOTW) - Stamps of the World Wiki has been created to allow information about philately to be freely available to collectors.
  • StampWorld - the largest on-line stamp catalogue
  • Colnect - (collect + connect ==> Colnect) is a comprehensive online solution for collectors of mass-produced collectibles (stamps, coins, phonecards and so many more) revolutionizing the collecting experience by providing online personal collection management within a designated community.
  • Woldwide stamp catalogues
  • Phildom (Spain)
