Bringing Dinosaurs back to life

Back to Exhibits Main page


"Bringing Dinosaurs back to life" by Jon Noad fom Canada, is a one frame philatelic exhibit, presented at the Calgary Stamp Show in 2019 (see on the right) where it honored by several awards:
  • Gold Medal of Calgary Philatelic Society
  • One Frame Grand Award of Calgary Philatelic Society
  • Thunderbird Award of NW Federation of Stamp Clubs
Even though the exhibit contain 8 A3 pages only it contain many unique philatelic objects such as original art works of stamp designers, rare postal stationery from 1922, stamps with mitted colors etc.

About the author: in June 2013 "Stamp Magazine" (philatelic magazine from Great Britain) published an interview with Jon Noad, where he presents himself and his hobby.
The interview can be read here.
