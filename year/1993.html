1993
Paleontology and Paleoanthropology related Philatelic items of the year 1993

Dinosaurs and other prehistoric animals, fossils, fossil-found places, paleontologists and contributors to Paleontology and Paleoanthropology science as well as Natural History Museums on stamps issued in 1993

Click on image to enlarge it or to be redirected to description page. Stamps on grey background are Undesired stamps

If you know about any other Philatelic item related to Paleontology or Paleoanthropology, issued this year which is not mentioned below, please inform me .



On June 11, 1993, the film Jurassic Park was released in the US.
The was the first time when the general public could see very realistic reconstructions of dinosaurs and other prehistoric reptiles.
Many Postal Authorities around the world used this movie as an opportunity to issue stamps depicting dinosaurs and other prehistoric animals.

Highlights of the year:

On October 10, three commonwealth countries, Australia, Canada, and New Zealand issued dinosaur stamps.
