Paleophilatelie Chronology based Catalog

Country based Catalog is here

Paleophilatelie.eu philatelic catalog is about Paleontology and Paleoanthropology in Philately

Currently this catalog shows images of over 1.000 stamp sets from almost 200 countries which are related to Paleontology and Paleoanthropology.
Some pages (2024-2015) shows other philatelic objects such as postmarks, meter-frankings and postal stationaries.
All included philatelic objects are shown in descending chronological order.

Note: The Chronology Catalog was designed first. Later, the Country Catalog was added. During the work on the country-based pages, additional stamps were discovered, mostly from the "Other stamps to consider" themes. Both catalogs are routinely updated at present.

Lists of the most recent stamps and announcement of feature issues are shown on the current year page and updated frequently.

Click on the thumbnail, on the year page, to see either a larger image or a description page for that item (if available).
Click on the country name to be redirected to the country page.
