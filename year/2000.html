2000
Paleontology and Paleoanthropology related Philatelic items of the year 2000

Dinosaurs and other prehistoric animals, fossils, fossil-found places, paleontologists and contributors to Paleontology and Paleoanthropology science as well as Natural History Museums on stamps issued in 2000

Click on image to enlarge it or to be redirected to description page. Stamps on grey background are Undesired stamps

If you know about any other Philatelic item related to Paleontology or Paleoanthropology, issued this year which is not mentioned below, please inform me .


This year 22 country issued some stamps depicting fossil and reconstruction of various prehistoric animals, include dinosaurs, and their footprints (stamps of Israel and Portugal); prehistoric plants, include petrified woods on stamps of Italy and Peru ; early humans, as well as some Paleoantropologists - Louis Leaky and Raymond Dart. Post Authorities of Mongolia and Uganda commemorate Charles Darwin on their Millenniums mini-sheets.
