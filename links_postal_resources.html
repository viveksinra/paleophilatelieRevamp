Worldwide Philatelic Resources: philatelic bureaus, stamp issue plans/programs and catalogs

This page contains links to websites of Postal Administrations around the world, official or country specialized philatelic bureaus, country specific stamp catalogs, stamp issue plans.


There are many administrations who have Philatelic Bureaus that will sell the country’s most recent stamps at face value to collectors. These bureaus may also have older stamps from past issues available at special prices. These links are organized below in a table in alphabetical order by country. The icons in the table are defined here:

Click on a flag icon to be redirected to home page of post office of a specific country. Click on this icon to see philatelic news of a specific country
