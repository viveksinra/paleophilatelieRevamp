About the Website

Paleophilatelie.eu is an online resource combining Philately with Paleontology and Paleoanthropology.

This website shows images and provides information about thousands of philatelic items related to Paleontology and Paleoanthropology from almost 200 countries and territories sorted by country and date of issue.

The website contains many hundreds of pages describing individual stamp issues and articles related to the "Paleophilatelie" topics, as well as many examples of philatelic exhibits.

If you are visiting the website for the first time, please read the "About Paleophilatelie.eu website" article to get an overview of the website.

The Paleophilatelie website can be explored on all types of electronic devices, including desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. However, the website is best viewed on a wide screen.

About the Team

The Paleophilatelie website is administered and owned solely by Mr. Michael Kogan from Germany, a member of the International Association of Philatelic Journalists and several international philatelic clubs.

Dr. Peter Voice of the Department of Geological and Environmental Sciences at Western Michigan University in the USA provides scientific advice and editorial support for the website, since 2019.

Awards

Since 2018, the "Paleophilatelie" website has regularly taken part in international competitions in the Electronic Literature category. The website has received numerous awards, including Large Gold Medals at the New Zealand National Philatelic Literature Exhibition in 2023 and 2025, and at the Great American Stamp Show in 2024.
